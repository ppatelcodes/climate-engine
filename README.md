# Climate Engine

## Overview

Climate Engine is a financial risk analytics platform designed to
quantify the impact of climate events on company fundamentals and
portfolio performance.

The platform integrates climate datasets, financial market data, and ESG
indicators to perform scenario analysis and stress testing aligned with
global regulatory expectations.

## Key Objectives

-   Link climate events (floods, cyclones, heatwaves) to financial
    outcomes
-   Measure sector and portfolio exposure to climate risk
-   Enable scenario-based stress testing using historical and simulated
    data
-   Provide decision-support insights for risk management and asset
    allocation

## Why This Project

Climate risk is increasingly being mandated by regulators such as SEC,
RBI, and EU authorities. Financial institutions are required to assess
and disclose climate-related risks.

This project demonstrates the ability to combine: - Finance and
portfolio theory - Climate data and macroeconomic signals - Data science
and quantitative modeling
